welcome to Spanish for Beginners

in this section you'll start learning the basic of the spanish language. from greetingd to grammer, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

Basic Spanish Phrases

  • Hello - Hola
  • How are you? - ¿Cómo estás?
  • Good morning - Buenos días
  • Thank you - Gracias
  • Goodbye - Adiós

Basic Grammar

Let’s start with the present tense of some common verbs.

Verb "Ser" (to be) in Present Tense:

  • Yo soy (I am)
  • Tú eres (You are)
  • Él/Ella es (He/She is)
  • Nosotros somos (We are)
  • Ellos/Ellas son (They are)

Essential vocabulary

Numbers (1-10):

  • 1 - uno
  • 2 - dos
  • 3 - tres
  • 4 - cuatro
  • 5 - cinco
  • 6 - seis
  • 7 - siete
  • 8 - ocho
  • 9 - nueve
  • 10 - diez
